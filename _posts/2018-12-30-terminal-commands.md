How to get keyboard event (shows keycodes etc..): 
`xev -event keyboard`

How to mod keymapping:
`xmodmap -e 'keycode <keycode>=<wheretomap>'`

Backlight:
`xbacklight -dec <amount>`
`xbacklight -inc <amount>`
